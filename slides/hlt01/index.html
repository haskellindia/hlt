HLT01: Getting Started

This material was created by a group of Haskell enthusiasts in the Haskell India Group (HIG). This group is an India-focussed Haskell Group on Telegram, meant primarily to connect Haskellers across India, and to complement existing personal meetups at various places in India. If you are interested to be a part of this group, and contribute to the growth of Haskell usage in India (either as a start-up, big company, independent professional, hobbyist, student, teacher / lecturer / professor, university, etc.), please join this group: https://t.me/haskellindia and help India!

Please feel free to share this material to anybody you think would be interested! And, if you would like to contribute to this learning trails, head here: https://github.com/haskellindia/haskellindia.github.io
